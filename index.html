Welcome to AIFL Documentation

AIFL (AI Foundational Language) is a symbolic language designed to represent and process AI-related concepts and operations.

Key Features of AIFL

  • Symbolic representation of AI operations
  • Support for complex logical expressions
  • Integration with natural language processing
  • Extensible framework for AI development
